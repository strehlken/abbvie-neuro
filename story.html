Section 02 · Deals

Two acquisitions

Almost every row above traces to one of two deals, with a handful of internal AbbVie programs (Vyalev, Duodopa, elezanumab) and a few partnerships filling the rest.

Allergan
Closed 8 May 2020 $63B

Vraylar, Botox Therapeutic, Ubrelvy, and atogepant (later Qulipta) all arrived with this deal, along with the legacy Saphris / Namenda / Namzaric lines that left the disclosure axis within a year.

Under AbbVie ownership the franchise has been driven by sNDA expansions: Vraylar bipolar depression (2019, last under Allergan), Vraylar MDD adjunct (Dec 2022), Qulipta chronic migraine (Apr 2023), and Qulipta acute migraine (submitted Sep 2025).

FY2025 disclosure for these four products: $9.7B.

Cerevel
Closed 1 Aug 2024 $8.7B

Four neuro programs at close: emraclidine (M4 PAM, lead asset), tavapadon (D1/D5 partial agonist), darigabat (GABA-A modulator), razpipadon (D1 follow-on).

Three months after close, emraclidine's Phase 2 EMPOWER schizophrenia trials read out negative (11 Nov 2024). AbbVie took a $3.5B impairment, kept emraclidine in development for AD psychosis and as an adjunctive schizophrenia therapy, and discontinued darigabat and razpipadon.

Tavapadon read out positive across TEMPO-1, -2, and -3 (Sep 2024 → AAN 2025); NDA submitted 26 Sep 2025; FDA decision expected H1 2026.
